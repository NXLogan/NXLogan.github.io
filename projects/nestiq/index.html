NestIQ

NestIQ is a real-estate research SaaS where AI does the legwork analysts used to do manually. Drop a neighbourhood, a budget range or an asset class — the platform returns scored listings, heat-mapped zones, comparable sales and yield projections in seconds, not spreadsheets.

The product splits cleanly between discovery and depth: a map-first view for spatial intelligence, a data grid for power users, and an AI brief that narrates why a property scores high or low. Teal accents mark opportunity, muted reds flag risk, and every filter state was designed to feel instant.

Research workflows drove the architecture — saved searches, alert rules, PDF exports for clients, team workspaces — wrapped in a dark interface that reads premium without feeling corporate. Built for agents, analysts and small funds who need institutional-grade research without institutional-grade friction.
