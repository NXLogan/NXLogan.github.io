Meridian AI

Meridian AI is a finance SaaS built around an AI copilot that doesn't sit in a chat bubble on the side — it drives the product. Cash runway, revenue forecasts, burn scenarios and anomaly detection are generated continuously from live data, then surfaced as actionable cards teams can drill into without leaving context.

The interface was designed for CFOs, ops leads and founders who live in numbers all day: emerald for healthy signals, crimson for risk, gold for AI insights. Treasury overview, expense breakdown, scenario modeling and investor-ready exports each occupy dedicated modules inside a cohesive dark shell.

Every screen was mapped as product design first — edge cases for empty states, stale data, alert fatigue — then translated into a Next.js stack with typed APIs and streaming AI responses. The result is a finance tool that feels like a Bloomberg terminal rebuilt for 2026: fast, opinionated and genuinely intelligent.
