Stratum

Stratum is an AI-native geopolitical intelligence platform — not a news aggregator with a map pin. Live conflict zones, sanctions trackers, election risk scores and alliance shift models feed an always-on analyst copilot that writes briefs in plain language for executives and policy teams.

The product splits between situational awareness and depth: a Mapbox-powered globe with heat layers, a timeline of escalations, scenario branches (base, escalation, de-escalation) and exportable PDF memos. Deep blue UI, crimson for flashpoints, amber for watch zones — hierarchy built for crisis rooms.

Every view was mapped for analyst workflows — saved regions, alert rules, team workspaces, source citations — wrapped in a dark interface that reads intelligence-grade without feeling military-generic.
