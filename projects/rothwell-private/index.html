Rothwell Private

Rothwell Private is a luxury investment portal for accredited investors accessing off-market real estate deals. Not a listing site — a private room. Deal pipeline, capital deployment tracking, LP reporting and secure document rooms each carry the weight the asset class demands.

Visual language follows old-money precision meets modern fintech: deep charcoal, brushed gold, restrained typography. Every property card shows yield, hold period, minimum ticket and risk tier at a glance. The AI layer surfaces portfolio drift, diversification gaps and timing signals without replacing human judgment.

Onboarding, KYC states, commitment flows and investor communications were designed as one continuous experience — no generic admin panels bolted on. A platform that feels as exclusive as the deals it hosts, built to earn trust in the first thirty seconds.
